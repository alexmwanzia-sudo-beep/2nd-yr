Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need a valid driver's license, credit card, and proof of insurance. Browse our available vehicles, select your preferred car, choose your rental period, and complete the booking process. Our team will guide you through each step.

What are your payment methods?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments including M-Pesa. Payment is required at the time of booking. We also offer flexible payment plans for long-term rentals.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours before the rental start time are fully refundable. Late cancellations may incur a fee. Special events and peak season bookings may have different cancellation terms.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes, we offer special rates for long-term rentals. Our long-term packages include maintenance, insurance, and 24/7 support. Contact our customer service team for personalized quotes and arrangements.

What happens in case of an accident?

In case of an accident, immediately contact our emergency support line. We'll guide you through the process, arrange for a replacement vehicle if needed, and handle all insurance claims. Our team is available 24/7 for assistance.
